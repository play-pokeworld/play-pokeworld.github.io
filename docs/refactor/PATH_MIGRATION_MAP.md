## Files intentionally not moved

Most original files under `src/legacy/scripts/**` and `src/localization/**` remain at their historical paths in this delivery. This reduces the chance of accidentally changing balancing/data behavior. The new Vite entry bundles them into a hashed production asset instead of requesting them one by one.
